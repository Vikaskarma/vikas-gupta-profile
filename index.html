Professional Summary

I am a Research Domain Expert at Navchetnna Agro Centre-Producer Company Limited, Mirzapur, with over 15 years of experience in circular bioeconomy and sustainability. My expertise lies in bioethanol production from lignocellulosic agro-waste, biochar generation for carbon sequestration and soil rejuvenation, and biomass valorization for clean energy and resource recovery. I lead initiatives to design decentralized, low-carbon bioprocessing systems that enhance rural livelihoods while aligning with carbon credit frameworks and ESG-aligned development goals, supporting India’s transition to a green economy. Previously, I led government- and industry-funded research projects on enzymatic hydrolysis, microbial fermentation, and phytoremediation at IILM University. I have published over 45 research papers and book chapters with global publishers like Springer and Taylor & Francis.

Career Objective: Seeking a dynamic role in sustainability and ESG innovation to apply my expertise in biotechnology, circular bioeconomy, and low-carbon solutions to drive impactful change and contribute to organizational goals.
